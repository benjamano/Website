Laser Tag Gun Repairs

As part of my work at Play2Day, I took on the challenge of repairing the faulty Laser-Tag guns, I had to teach myself how to repair through trial and error. I was commited to this as I wanted to make a proper change to Play2Day, as it meant alot to me personally.

The video here shows a timelapse of me repairing a laser gun, this involved switching the shell (Case) of the gun. This is the most complicated procedure, mainly because it requries everything to be un-hot-glued. This usually takes about an hour and requires patience and knowledge on what you're doing. Keep in mind I had to full teach myself how to do this!!
